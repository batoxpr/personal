about me
I like everything creative!

Hey there! I'm Bato, a new media artist from Mongolia. I'm currently a rising junior at New York University's Abu Dhabi campus, where I'm pursuing a degree in Interactive Media Arts and Music.

I am passionate about exploring the intersection between creative technology, visual arts, sound, and interaction design. Throughout my journey as an artist, I've gained experience with a range of new media software, including Ableton Live, JavaScript, P5.JS, Arduino, Adobe Creative Cloud, and Twine. Combining my skills, I aim to create innovative and engaging works that inspire and connect with others on a deeper level.

projects
madcap mansion

Madcap Mansion is an interactive, story-driven game about a group of college students getting trapped in a haunted mansion. The game was made on Twine using JavaScript, CSS, and HTML.

emoji war

Emoji War is a shoot-em-up arcade game made after the design of old-school games in similar styles. It was developed on the P5 library of JavaScript.

film premiere site

"Morning in Dubai & Karachi" is a short film made by Bato and Adullah for their Communications Lab class with professor Michael Allison.

maya in abu dhabi

"Maya in Abu Dhabi" is an interactive comics about the life experiences of NYU Abu Dhabi freshmen. It utilizes various sound interactions and visual elements, which were designed on Procreate.

stories of the unusual

"Stories of the Unsual" is a radio show hosted on an interactive website. The intention was to reimagine a radio show experience with the elevation of modern web interaction.

comm lab games

"Comm Lab Games" is a fictional documentary, which re-imaged the story of viral TV series "Squid Games" in the context of college students.
